Team

Welcome to our team page! We are a group of passionate developers who are committed to making a positive impact on the environment through the use of virtual reality technology. Our team consists of talented individuals who bring a diverse set of skills and experiences to the table.We are united by our belief in the power of technology to make our planet a greener, more sustainable place. At the heart of our work are the virtual reality platforms that we create. These platforms enable users to visualize and practicalize climate sustainability, making it easier for people to understand the impact of their actions and make informed decisions about their behavior. We are proud of the work we do and the impact we are making. We believe that by working together, we can build a better future for our planet and all of its inhabitants. Frequently Asked Questions

Most frequently asked questions are answered below. There a certain answers given to the questions asked most often.

  • How can virtual reality fight climate changes?

    In 2017, WWF in collaboration with Lenovo and Google gave life to an amazing project that combines virtual and real world with the aims of both raising awareness and pushing people to do concrete actions. With its VR game �Into the Wild�, in fact, WWF gave the chance to the gamers to take real actions also outside the game: gamers for example can plant trees in the game and for every tree planted, a real one can be planted in the real world.

  • Can virtual reality Inspire action on climate changes?

    Virtual Reality could inspire humans through the uses of oculus headset to help create and develop a better greener planet.Most people wonder why virtual reality could be used to inspire humans, as the name implies virtual reality, which means a reality which has the capability to capture ones mind and inspire them to help sustain climate changes.

  • How does Virtual reality reduces the need to move with transportation?

    Augmented Reality can also contribute to minimizing transport gas emissions. During the pandemic we have seen how much we can actually do without going out of our house especially in terms of entertainment experiences, such as going to the cinema or theatres.

  • How deos Virtual reality work with climate advocacy?

    We use Oculus Go headsets with free, pre-written applications to teach the science and solutions of climate change. Using Google Street View, pupils can dive into healthy, bleached, and dead coral reefs and compare the difference in their ecosystem structures.

  • Can Virtual reality save the planet?

    Yes, because you are in a one-to-one relationship with space. It's an incredible tool that can be used to see larger scale in small units. The Virtual reality technology is one of the latest technology used to solve and innovate lots of products used to save the planet earth.
